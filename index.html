Wayfarer

Rules for science fiction roleplaying.

Manifesto

Ignore everything you know about Star Wars except for the original movie. Flush from your mind all notions of prequels or sequels or paperback novels or lackluster MMOFPS games or fan-maintained wiki drama.

Recall how you felt while watching the binary sunset for the first time. I cannot speak for you, but my own feelings mirrored Luke's yearning to answer the call to adventure. A universe ripe with potential had just been established. Tantalizing hints of ancient glory, alien cultures, pernicious evil, and heroic struggles awaited us.

That's the feeling I mean to evoke.
